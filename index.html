DHM (dihydromyricetin) has been the active ingredient in Korean hangover recovery drinks for over 20 years. We took the same proven formula and reformatted it into a 15ml stick pouch you can carry anywhere.

A 2023 USC human trial showed DHM significantly reduces hangover severity by accelerating the breakdown of acetaldehyde, the toxic byproduct of alcohol metabolism responsible for most symptoms.
