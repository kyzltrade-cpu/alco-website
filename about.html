How It Started

THE MORNING
AFTER PROBLEM.

We weren't trying to build a supplements company. We were just tired of the trade-off — the one where you either skip the birthday dinner, the client drinks, the Saturday out with friends, or you pay for it the next day.

Kyle, Will, and Randev had all read the research on DHM. They'd seen it work in Korean hangover recovery drinks. But nothing existed that actually fit the way people lived — something small enough to carry anywhere, fast enough to take in the moment, and effective enough to actually matter the next morning.

So they built alco. A 15ml jelly stick. Peach plum. DHM 200mg. No faff. For people who want to live fully without sacrificing the next day.
